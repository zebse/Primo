Our Products Quality Specifications

Delivery of top quality products to our customers ensured by having full quality control and maintenance systems in place are key success factors of Premot. Premo use of the new technology of dry hulling in Ethiopia (hence making premo the pioneer of (dry ) hulling sesame in Ethiopia) thereby ensuring local value-addition, which is very beneficial for the economy as a whole, premo also follows an integrated Internal Control System and works according to international food safety and quality systems like HACCP and ISO 22000. Premo Trading process cleaned purity is 99.5 % and hulled 99.8 and color sort purity is about 99.9.
