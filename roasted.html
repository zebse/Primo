Roasted Products

ROASTED SESAME

Roasted sesame seeds are small, golden-brown seeds with a rich, nutty flavor and crunchy texture. They are commonly used as a topping in various dishes or as a flavorful ingredient in snacks and baked goods.

ROASTED FLAXSEED

Roasted flaxseeds are small, dark-brown seeds with a nutty aroma and a crispy texture. They are prized for their enhanced flavor profile and nutritional benefits, often enjoyed as a wholesome snack or sprinkled over salads, yogurt, or oatmeal for added crunch and omega-3 fatty acids.

NIGER SEED

Niger seeds, also known as black sesame seeds, are tiny, oil-rich seeds with a nutty flavor. They add a delightful crunch and a touch of visual interest to baked goods, salads, and various dishes.

SUNFLOWER SEEDS

Sunflowers, with their bright yellow petals and cheerful faces, are giant daisies that turn their heads throughout the day to follow the sun. These tall beauties not only brighten gardens but also produce delicious, protein-packed seeds.
