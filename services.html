Our Services

Ethiopia is the largest and organic producer of White sesame seed in the world with annual production of 350,000 Metric Tone (2016) and with an average of annual growth of 6%. In Ethiopia, we have 2 types of sesame seeds. Whitish Humera-Gondar sesame seed and Wollega sesame seeds" White Sesame seed grows in the North and North Western parts of Ethiopia (Humera, Gondar and Metema) and Western Wollega.
The Humera-Gondar variety is coveted worldwide for its aroma and sweet taste. It is suitable for various bakery products and is applied for Tahnia, Halewat and Hulling. It is also used for oil crushing. This type can grow in Humera, Gondar and Metema.
The Wollega variety The major competitive advantage of this type is its high oil content and is applied for oil crushing. It can be prepared as a snack on its own but is mostly combined and prepared with Qolo. It is found in West Wollega zone, Assosa and bure.

Elephant Grass

Elephant grass or Napier grass is an important crop that provides good fodder for grazing animals and dairy farmers. Being an important food item for African elephants, it’s named ‘Elephant grass’. The grass is rough, hairy, green, yellow or purple-coloured that can grow ~15 feet tall. The leaves are narrow and have a white central vein, pointed tips, sparse hairs and razor-sharp edges that are impenetrable. The grass has multi-branched stems that often grow in dense, bamboo-like clumps.

Quality Assurance

Cleaning Process.

Control of the raw sesame is initiated right from the farm level to ensure that all raw materials are only packaged in clean polypropylene bags. Clean trucks are used and the trucks are not opened during transport.

Sorting Process

Samples are drawn which are sent for checking for residue analysis and the raw material is also checked for moisture level, purity etc. The raw material from the warehouse stored in lots is divided into batches.

De-Hulling Process

The cleaned sesame is emptied into the peeler tank, which has a capacity of 19 MT. All machines are linked together using pneumatic System. which scoop the seeds, thereby avoiding breaking of seeds during movements from one machine to another.

Roasting Process

The next stage in the hulling process makes use of an air filter that filters air into a blower, with the air proceeding into the heater, where the air is heated and transported into the dryer.
