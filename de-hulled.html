Cleaned and De-hulled Products

DEHULLED SUNFLOWER SEED

De-hulled sunflower seeds have the tough outer shell removed, leaving just the soft, edible kernel. This kernel has a mild, nutty flavor and is commonly used in snacks, baked goods, and as a nutritious ingredient in various food products.

DEHULLED SESAME SEEDS

Dehulled sesame seeds are the inner kernel of the sesame seed with the outer hull or shell removed. This process leaves behind the soft, white, and flavorful sesame seed that is commonly used in cooking, baking, and as a nutritious snack.
