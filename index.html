Our Services

CLEANING

The control of our products begins at the farm level, ensuring packaging in clean polypropylene bags and transport in sealed trucks. Samples are checked for residue, moisture, and purity, and raw materials are stored in batches. Each batch is moved to the cleaning line, and the original bags are disposed of after emptying

DE-HULLING

De-hulling is a process commonly used in the agricultural industry to remove the outer husk or hull from grains, seeds, or nuts, leaving behind the edible kernel or seed inside. This process is essential for improving the quality and marketability of agricultural products, as the hulls often contain indigestible fibers, bitter compounds, or undesirable flavors

ROASTING

Roasting is a cooking method that involves heating food, typically in an oven or over an open flame, using dry heat. It's commonly used for a variety of foods, including vegetables, meats, nuts, and coffee beans.During roasting, the dry heat causes caramelization and Maillard reactions, which result in the browning of the food's surface, creating rich flavors and aromas.

SORTING

Sorting or Grading in the seeds export process involves the systematic arrangement and separation of seeds based on various criteria such as size, shape, color, maturity, and quality. This step is crucial in ensuring that only high-quality seeds are exported, meeting the standards and requirements of both domestic and international markets.
