About Us

Established in 2024, PRIMO AGRO INDUSTRIAL TRADING PLC ranks amongst the leading Exporting firms in the field of Agro based Commodities and food products in Ethiopia. Our ceaseless pursuit to provide quality services and prompt facilities are guided by the values of professionalism, honesty and efficiency. This has earned us our success and strong position in the competitive international arena.

Vision

Setting up a top-of-the–range quality HACCP operated, organic certified hulling factory (using the dry/mechanical hulling method), with the capacity to produce 1, 500 – 1,700 kg per hour of organic hulled sesame seeds. Initiating the internationally certified Quality of sesame, cotton, soya beans and mung beans,Pulses and grains. Specfically Quality sesame seeds will collects from with an out growers or Suppplier from Humera and Wollega Region.

Mission

PRIMO AGRO INDUSTRIAL TRADING PLC is to be the world food and bakery industry’s premier supplier of top quality, competitively priced, certified Quality seeds for a healthier living.

Why Choose Us

Product Quality

Understanding customer requirements for quality in each industry, and thus delivering "pure 100% natural organic products" that meets the prerequisites and characteristics of each order.

Speed

Guaranteed speed through logistics and strategic positioning, allowing goods to reach customer hands efficiently and on time.

Customer Service

Special importance to excellent Customer Service as not to lose clients to our competitors. Our team is always ready to take orders, provide assistance and service to our client’s requests.

Safety

Above par safety precautions, we ensure strong packaging measures in shipping goods both aircargo and sea shipments overseeing the entire process avoiding spillage, from loading to arrival at final destination.
